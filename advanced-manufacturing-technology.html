SECTOR 03 · DRIVING INNOVATION

Advanced Manufacturing & Technology

An environment where ideas can be developed, tested and commercialised — built on an internationally recognised manufacturing base.

Rail Electronics Engineering Robotics

Innovation sits at the heart of Wiltshire's economy, and advanced manufacturing is one of its clearest expressions. The county's manufacturing base includes internationally recognised businesses working in rail, electronics, engineering and robotics — organisations that combine established industrial expertise with genuine investment in research and development.

From advanced engineering to emerging technologies, Wiltshire provides an environment where ideas can move from concept to commercial product, supported by a skilled workforce and a strong culture of applied innovation.

For investors, this represents an opportunity to access a mature but forward-looking manufacturing ecosystem — one with the technical depth to support serious R&D and production activity.

WHY IT MATTERS

Supports the "Driving Innovation" pillar

From advanced engineering and manufacturing to scientific research and robotics, Wiltshire provides an environment where ideas can be developed, tested and commercialised — the practical engine room of the county's innovation story.
