SECTOR 04 · GROWING SUSTAINABLE PROSPERITY

Agri-Tech & Food Innovation

Wiltshire's agricultural landscape provides real opportunities for innovation in food production, technology and environmental sustainability.

Agri-Tech Food Production Sustainability

Wiltshire combines economic growth with long-term sustainability, and agri-tech is central to that ambition. The county's agricultural landscape provides opportunities for innovation across food production, agricultural technology and environmental sustainability — building on a rural economy that is increasingly technology-led.

This is not innovation happening in spite of Wiltshire's rural character; it's innovation that grows directly out of it, drawing on genuine agricultural expertise combined with emerging technology and research capability.

For investors, this represents an opportunity to work at the intersection of traditional agriculture and modern technology, in a county with the land, expertise and ambition to support that transition.

WHY IT MATTERS

Supports "Growing Sustainable Prosperity"

Wiltshire has significant opportunities in agri-tech, food innovation, clean growth and the transition to a lower-carbon economy — combining growth with genuine long-term sustainability.
