SECTOR 01 · PROTECTING THE FUTURE

Defence, Security & Cyber

Wiltshire plays a nationally significant role in defence, security and cyber resilience — a position few UK locations can credibly claim.

Ministry of Defence Dstl Defence Digital Cyber

Security and defence provide the strongest unifying theme within Wiltshire's economy, and sit at the heart of the Invest in Wessex proposition. The presence of the Ministry of Defence, Defence Digital, the Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (Dstl), and a growing ecosystem of businesses working across defence, cyber and advanced technologies gives Wiltshire a position that few places in the UK can credibly claim.

Rather than treating security as a sector in its own right, Invest in Wessex presents it as a cross-cutting strength — one that underpins opportunities across health resilience, advanced manufacturing and technology, and the wider innovation ecosystem.

For businesses and investors, this means access to specialist expertise, established supply chains, and proximity to organisations operating at the forefront of national security and defence technology.

WHY IT MATTERS

Supports the "Protecting the Future" pillar

Through organisations such as the Ministry of Defence, Dstl, Defence Digital and UKHSA, Wiltshire contributes directly to national resilience, security and preparedness — a role that extends far beyond the county's boundaries.
