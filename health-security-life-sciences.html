SECTOR 02 · PROTECTING THE FUTURE

Health Security & Life Sciences

Wiltshire's strengths in vaccine development and biosecurity are nationally significant, providing a strong foundation for health-focused innovation and investment.

UKHSA Biosecurity Vaccine Development Health Resilience

Alongside its defence and security strengths, Wiltshire has established and growing capabilities in life sciences and health security. The county's role in vaccine development and biosecurity is nationally significant, anchored by the UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) and a wider network of organisations working on health resilience and preparedness.

This gives Wiltshire a distinctive position within the UK's life sciences landscape — not as a generalist location, but as a place with genuine depth in a specific and increasingly important area of national capability.

For investors and businesses, this creates opportunities to access specialist expertise, partnerships and infrastructure connected to some of the country's most important health security work.

WHY IT MATTERS

Supports both "Protecting" and "Driving Innovation"

Health security sits at the intersection of two of Wiltshire's narrative pillars — contributing to national resilience while also representing one of the county's strongest areas of scientific and technological innovation.
