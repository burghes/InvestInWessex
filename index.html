STRATEGIC POSITIONING

A story that hasn't yet been told clearly enough.

Wiltshire has a strong economic story to tell, but it is not yet widely understood or consistently articulated. Too often the county is viewed through the lens of its proximity to larger cities, or as a predominantly rural area. While both are part of the picture, neither reflects the scale of innovation, research, advanced industry and nationally significant activity already taking place here.

Invest in Wessex exists to establish a clear and credible investment proposition that reflects the county's real strengths and builds confidence among investors, businesses, developers, government and partners. This is not a new identity for Wiltshire — it is the county's existing strengths, brought together into one coherent narrative.

Wiltshire is home to globally recognised organisations operating at the forefront of defence, security, health protection, advanced manufacturing and agri-technology. These are not emerging aspirations; they are the foundation for what comes next.

KEY MESSAGES

What we want investors to understand.

A county of innovation

From health security and advanced manufacturing to defence technologies and agri-tech, Wiltshire is home to organisations developing solutions to some of the UK's most significant challenges — a place where ideas are developed, tested, commercialised and scaled.

Nationally significant

Few places can demonstrate the concentration of nationally important organisations found within Wiltshire. The county contributes directly to the UK's security, resilience and future prosperity.

Built for collaboration

Successful innovation increasingly relies on collaboration between businesses, government, research institutions and investors. Wiltshire brings together the people, organisations and ideas that drive progress.

Supports business growth

The focus is not simply on attracting investment, but on creating the conditions in which businesses — new and existing — can innovate, expand and succeed over the long term.

Exceptional quality of place

Wiltshire combines economic opportunity with a high quality of life, attractive market towns, a skilled workforce, and strong connections to major economic centres.

Shaping the future economy

Wiltshire's priority sectors align closely with national growth priorities — defence, life sciences, advanced manufacturing, agri-tech and clean growth — positioning the county at the forefront of sectors that will define the UK's future.

WHO WE'RE TALKING TO

Audiences that shape investment decisions

Communications are focused on the audiences most likely to contribute to investment, business expansion and long-term economic prosperity — not a broad, untargeted approach.

Existing businesses & major employers — the majority of economic growth comes from businesses already based in Wiltshire, not relocations.

Potential investors — UK and international businesses considering investment, R&D activity or relocation into priority sectors.

Developers & property investors — critical to delivering the employment and innovation space growth depends on.

Strategic partners — government, universities, research institutions and industry networks who extend the proposition's reach and credibility.

Talent & future workforce — businesses invest where they can access skilled people.
