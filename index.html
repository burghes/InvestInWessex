STRATEGIC POSITIONING

A region-wide story, not a county-by-county one.

Wessex brings together some of southern England's most significant economic assets — nationally important defence and security infrastructure, a globally recognised life sciences base, critical energy and food production capacity, a major financial services cluster, and a growing digital and advanced engineering economy — all within Wiltshire, Somerset, Dorset and BCP.

As devolution reshapes how this region is governed and promoted, Invest in Wessex brings these strengths together into one coherent investment story: not a new identity, but a clearer articulation of what's already here, and what it adds up to at scale.

Home to 1.9 million people and 80,000 enterprises, Wessex is a place where outstanding landscapes and World Heritage Sites sit alongside globally significant industry — where the ancient and the modern are united by excellence.
