SECTOR 05 · GROWING SUSTAINABLE PROSPERITY

Net Zero & Green Economy

Wiltshire is positioned to play a genuine role in the UK's transition to a lower-carbon economy.

Clean Growth Net Zero Green Economy

Alongside its established strengths, Wiltshire is building genuine capability in clean growth and the transition to a lower-carbon economy. This sits naturally alongside the county's agri-tech strengths, sharing the same commitment to combining economic growth with long-term environmental sustainability.

Rather than treating net zero as a compliance requirement, Invest in Wessex positions it as a growth opportunity in its own right — one where businesses developing genuinely sustainable technologies and practices can find a supportive environment in which to scale.

For investors, this is an emerging but credible part of the county's proposition, aligned with national priorities around decarbonisation and green growth.

WHY IT MATTERS

Supports "Growing Sustainable Prosperity"

Net Zero and the Green Economy align closely with national growth priorities, positioning Wiltshire at the forefront of sectors expected to drive future investment, productivity and innovation across the UK.
